Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

ColorCloset (“the app”, “we”) helps you put together office and casual outfits from the colours already in your wardrobe. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles. The short version: your wardrobe stays on your phone, and we run no servers and no analytics.

At a glance
  • No account to create. No tracking. No ads. No data sold or shared.
  • Your wardrobe, saved looks and history live on your device.
  • Backup is optional and goes to your own Google Drive — we can’t see it.
  • Reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

What the app stores on your device

The colours you own, your skin-tone selection, saved looks, worn history, and your settings (theme, reminder time) are stored locally on your phone. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up.

Google Sign-In & Google Drive backup (optional)

If you tap “Connect Google Drive”, the app uses Google Sign-In so it can save and restore a single backup file in your own Google Drive. When you do this:

  • We request the “see, edit, create and delete only the specific files you use with this app” permission (the Google drive.file scope). The app can only access the one backup file it creates in a “ColorCloset” folder — it cannot see any of your other Drive files.
  • Your Google account’s email/name is used only to show you which account is connected. It is stored on your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • The backup contains your wardrobe data and is stored in your Drive, under your control. We have no servers and never receive or store a copy.

You can disconnect at any time in Backup & restore → sign out, and you can delete the backup file directly from your Google Drive (or by re-backing up after clearing your data).

Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device at the time you choose. These reminders are generated on-device and send no data anywhere.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not run analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not operate a server that collects your data.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives on your device (and optionally in your own Drive), you control it entirely. To remove it: use Reset wardrobe in the app, uninstall the app, and/or delete the ColorCloset backup file from your Google Drive. There is no account on our side to delete.

Children

ColorCloset is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Third-party services

The optional backup feature relies on Google Drive and Google Sign-In, governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we’ll update the date above and post the revised version at this URL.
